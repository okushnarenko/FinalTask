ABOUT US


About IEC Partners Facts

International Exhibition Centre is the biggest specialised exhibition complex in the Baltic countries. We provide event space easy transformable to a necessary amount and arrangement of rooms to satisfy client’s needs, mobile retractable theatre seating and event support services by a dedicated support team to a variety of large, technically challenging and complex events.

Professional industry associations appreciate the trade shows held by BT 1 and contribution to this business sector. BT 1 works side by side with more than 60 industry associations producing different joint events – conferences, seminars, master classes, presentations – held during trade shows. Associations tend to choose trade shows a place to hold their events and meetings, which is an essential complement to the event content.

The International Exhibition Company BT 1 takes pride in its more than 22-years long experience in exhibition business. During these years, BT 1 has become the leading exhibition organiser in the Baltic states, organising major exhibitions in food and construction industries, beauty, tourism, education, engineering, metal processing and other industries.

The number of foreign companies in exhibitions organised by BT 1 throughout the year makes up approx. 25 % but the number of foreign visitors, according to approximate estimates, about 20 %.
